A Full Service Construction Company

We Rehab Homes & Rebuild Lives.

Reconstruction Technology Partners (RTP), a Safer Social Enterprises Venture, is a full-service construction company that provides on-the-job training for workers who have arrest or conviction records. RTP will provide access to a state of the art training facility and tailored instruction for individuals to gain in-demand skills within the construction trade. The goal of RTP is to strengthen communities, provide training and employment to people with criminal records and create unrestricted revenue for Safer.

In addition to our licensed and bonded professional services, your business goes towards helping men and women from the community gain employment & launch careers. Doing business with us also means your dollar circulates in the community longer, which builds wealth and changes lives.
